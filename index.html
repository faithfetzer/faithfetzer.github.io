About Me

I have always loved learning new things, teaching, helping people, problem-solving and making experiences better for everyone. In the past, this often meant working with other teams or leaders to streamline workflows. With the goal to be more efficent and accurate, training people on them, and finding tracking methods, I always want to make it so that the customer and employee experience are both improving. I always work to make things better, looking ways to improve, large and small.

A few years ago, I found my way to coding, and I realized how this is the perfect tool to combine my passions- problem-solving, teaching, creating. I started learning to code on my own through programs online, and after some time dedicated to the basics, I am currently working through the Web Development Program at Eleven Fifty Academy, with expected completion in July 2021.

In my free time? I create pottery and teach classes- the Indianapolis arts community is amazing and I am always looking for new ways to be involved and give back. I love to travel and explore new places. I am a runner, triathlete, hiker. And I am often finding a new hobby or some new rabbit hole to dive down doing research.
